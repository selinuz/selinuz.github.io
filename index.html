About Me

I’m a senior Computer Science student at UBC, passionate about solving everyday problems with creative solutions, managing meaningful projects, and collaborating with others to bring ideas to life.

I’m honored to have received the Karen McKellin International Leader of Tomorrow Award , a full-ride scholarship that recognized my academic achievements and leadership potential during high school. This opportunity allowed me to move from my home country, Türkiye, to Canada to pursue my education and has further motivated me to push boundaries and strive for meaningful impact.

Outside of academics, I enjoy sailing for its perfect mix of adventure and calm. I’m an avid reader of philosophy, feminist literature, and history, which help me think critically. Photography is another passion, letting me capture meaningful moments and appreciate everyday details.
